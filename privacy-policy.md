\## Introduction



Time Sync - NTP Clock ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our browser extension handles data and your privacy rights.

\## Data Collection and Usage



\### Information We DO NOT Collect



Time Sync - NTP Clock does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any of the following:



\- Personal identification information (name, email, address, phone number)

\- Browsing history or activity

\- User credentials or passwords

\- Location data beyond what you voluntarily configure

\- Cookies or tracking identifiers

\- Analytics or usage statistics

\- Device information or system specifications

\- IP addresses for tracking purposes



\### Information Stored Locally



The extension stores the following settings \*\*LOCALLY on your device only\*\* using the browser's local storage API:



\- \*\*NTP Server Address:\*\* Your preferred time server (e.g., pool.ntp.org)

\- \*\*Auto-Sync Interval:\*\* Your chosen automatic synchronization frequency (in seconds)

\- \*\*Last Sync Timestamp:\*\* The time of your last successful synchronization

\- \*\*Auto-Sync Status:\*\* Whether automatic synchronization is enabled or disabled

\- \*\*Window Preferences:\*\* Display settings for detached window mode (if applicable)



\*\*Important:\*\* This data:

\- Never leaves your device

\- Is not transmitted to any server

\- Is not accessible to the developer

\- Can be deleted by uninstalling the extension or clearing browser data



\## Network Requests



\### Time Synchronization Services



The extension makes network requests \*\*ONLY\*\* for the purpose of fetching accurate time data from the following trusted time services:



1\. \*\*WorldTimeAPI\*\* (worldtimeapi.org)

2\. \*\*TimeAPI.io\*\* (timeapi.io)

3\. \*\*Google Public NTP\*\* (google.com)

4\. \*\*NIST Time Services\*\* (nist.gov)



\### Data Sent in Network Requests



When synchronizing time, the extension sends:

\- Standard HTTP/HTTPS request headers (required for web communication)

\- No personal information

\- No tracking identifiers

\- No user-specific data



\### Data Received



The extension receives:

\- Current UTC time information

\- Server response headers (including date/time stamps)

\- Standard HTTP response data



All received data is used solely to display accurate time and is not stored permanently.



\## Permissions Explanation



The extension requests the following permissions, which are used exclusively as described:



\### 1. Storage Permission

\- \*\*Purpose:\*\* To save your preferences (NTP server, sync interval) locally on your device

\- \*\*Scope:\*\* Local storage only

\- \*\*Data Access:\*\* Settings you configure within the extension



\### 2. Host Permissions

The extension requires access to the following domains for time synchronization:



\- `https://worldtimeapi.org/\*` - WorldTimeAPI time service

\- `https://timeapi.io/\*` - TimeAPI.io time service

\- `https://www.google.com/\*` - Google time services

\- `https://www.nist.gov/\*` - NIST (National Institute of Standards and Technology) time services



\*\*Important:\*\* These permissions are used ONLY to fetch time data. The extension does not:

\- Access content from other websites you visit

\- Modify web pages

\- Inject scripts into other sites

\- Read or modify your browsing data
